About Principal

Founded in 1879, we are Principal - a global investment management leader. We offers businesses, individuals and institutional clients a wide range of financial products and services, which includes retirement, asset management and insurance, through our diverse family of financial services companies.

A member of the FORTUNE 500, we have over half a trillion dollars in assets under management with around 20 million customers worldwide, whom we serve through a national (U.S.) network of financial professionals and a diverse family of companies with offices in 18 countries, throughout Asia, Australia, Europe, Latin America, and North America.

Principal is traded on the New York Stock Exchange under the symbol PFG.
