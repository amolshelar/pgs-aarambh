We Care


We care for our most valued assets Our People.
Be it picking them from home for office, or sharing their canteen bills, to flexible working hours, we are consistently enhancing the overall employee experience.
We also care for their overall well-being, be it their PHYSICAL WELLBEING, EMOTIONAL WELLBEING OR FINANCIAL WELLBEING.
As we embark on the journey of great place to work, we have been consistently bringing different programs to ensure holistic well-being of our one Principal family.


Prerana

Prerana in PGS is one such initiative by which women from different arenas and life stages from PGS have come together, to re-discover healthy work life balance while evolving as role models themselves and inspiring other women colleagues.  
Launched in July 2015, the areas of focus are: | Career progression | Career options while balancing needs on home front | Support System – Internal | External | Wellbeing – Health | Finance | Celebrations |


Sparsh

Sparsh is a android based mobile application for our female colleagues who are on maternity leave.
With Sparsh, they are not only connected to PGS through various regular updates they can also do lot of transactions like insurance, connecting to HR, connecting to their buddy through this App. Sparsh also provides details about exercises, food habits to be followed during pregnancy and also information about near by child care centers
