Principal Global Services

Started in 2006, Principal Global Services (PGS) is a Global In-house Center of the Principal Financial Group. PGS plays a significant role in supporting The Principal by a variety of services like IT (Information Technology), IS (Information Services), ITeS (IT Enabled Services) and BES (Business Enabling Services).

Businesses We Support

We provide IT solutions and services across all our business lines - Retirement, Asset Management and Insurance, in US and International markets across Asia and Latin America. We support a wide range of business and knowledge processes ranging from 401(k) pensions, claims processing to underwriting support for our various retirement and insurance businesses. Close interaction with the business teams and exposure to their strategy make for a great learning experience.

Culture

An organization with open culture and very approachable leaders make our team like a close-knit family. Employee friendly policies that are consistent across levels help employees maintain healthy work-life Balance. Engagement and empowerment create a vibrant workplace. Ethics and Integrity play a significant role in our organization and we feel proud to demonstrate it, every single day.

What We Do?

We handle large, high-impact and strategic projects independently. Through a spectrum of roles in Architecture, Design, Project Management, Business Analysis, Business Intelligence, Data Analytics and Predictive Modeling, we make a positive impact on our businesses. Our strong partnership with the business teams ensures we are accountable for outcome and not just output. Accounting Community

support ranges from routine to high end accounting in areas of Business Valuation, Corporate Tax, Financial Reporting, Reconciliations, SOX Testing and Audit Assistance.

Career Opportunities

Being part of a global organization, we play global roles including Leadership roles. Career progression is well defined, transparent and provides various paths to unleash the potential as well as allows flexibility to switch paths. Paths are available for employees with pure technology inclination, or business/domain inclination, as well as broad management inclination. Leadership development is a key focus in the organization and is pursued through various programs.

PGS Vision for the Future

Having successfully accomplished the initial program objectives, PGS leadership has a vision for the future. PGS will be:

The best employer in India where employees, working as a vibrant and cohesive team, are a part of the global talent pool; The partner of choice for the Principal Financial Group (world-wide), providing best-in-class, end-to-end solutions across all ranges of products; and A great corporate citizen
