Principal Global Services (PGS)

Started in 2006, Principal Global Services (PGS) is a Global In-house Center of the Principal . It plays significant role in supporting Principal by a variety of services like IT (Information Technology), IS (Information Services), ITeS (IT Enabled Services) and BES (Business Enabling Services).

Businesses We Support

We provide IT services across all our business lines - Retirement, Asset Management and Insurance, in US and international locations.

What We Do?

We handle large, high-impact and strategic projects independently through spectrum of roles in Architecture, Design, Project Management, Business Analysis, Business Intelligence, Data Analytics and Predictive Modeling making positive impact on our businesses. We also support corporate functions like HR, business valuation, corporate tax, financial reporting, corporate real estate and aviation and so on.

Culture

An organization with open culture and very approachable leaders make our team like a close-knit family. Employee friendly policies that are consistent across levels help employees maintain healthy work-life Balance. Engagement and empowerment create a vibrant workplace. Ethics and Integrity play a significant role in our organization and we feel proud to demonstrate it every single day.

Career Opportunities

Being part of a global organization, we play global roles including Leadership roles. Career progression is well defined, transparent and provides various paths to unleash the potential as well as allows flexibility to switch paths. Paths are available for employees with pure technology inclination, or business/domain inclination, as well as broad management inclination. Leadership development is a key focus in the organization and is pursued through various programs.
