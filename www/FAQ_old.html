FAQ

Does Principal have work from home culture?
Principal doesn’t have work from home culture but based on requirement/ exceptions few Business Units support 24x7.

What is office schedule?
Office schedule is either 1030 hrs to 1930 hrs or 0900 hrs to 1800 hrs based on employee needs. He/she may change office timing as needed.


What are timings for breakfast and lunch?
There are no specific timings. You may go for it as per your convenience.


If I have any concerns or questions whom should I contact?
You may contact buddy assigned to you/ you may contact HR department/ Leaders or their superior/s.


What are different components of my salary?
You will get to know that at the time of induction. HR will give you all the information and there will be dedicated session to address your queries regarding same.


Will I get any accommodation till the time I don’t get a house to live in Pune?
Principal will give a week’s accommodation to all the new joiners. In this weeks’ time you can finalize a flat/house for yourself.
